Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy bechamel. It's hearty, comforting and perfect for feeding a crowd.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion
  2. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce and bechamel in a baking dish
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese
  6. Bake at 180°C for 45 minutes until golden
